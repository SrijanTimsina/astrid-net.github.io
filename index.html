Astrid is a team that aims to use blockchain technology to develop effective and technologically advanced solutions. We do this by offering different types of services.

What is Astrid?

Astrid is a software development team born just over a year ago in Puglia, Italy.

Our experience in the field of programming is many years, with a strong training in the field of Data. You can see some of our previous work on GitHub.

A little over a year ago we learned about Blockchain Technology. The idea of decentralization and the concept of ownership of data in a unique way immediately fascinated us. From that day to now, our goal has never changed:
Every day we explore new ways to connect Blockchain with Data Science and close the gap with centralized technologies.

What services does Astrid offer?

We currently offer the following services:

•Data Providing We are going to partner with ChainLink - the big player in oracles field - to become Data Providers on their protocol. Take a look at the Data Providing section to find out the data you can get through Astrid.

•Consulting Our Data Science and Blockchain expertise allows us to develop any type of customized service you need. Visit the Consulting section to see some of our work and find out how we can help you.

•Apps We are designing an App in order to improve the data analysis of dApps in a secure, decentralized and anonymous way. We are still in an early-stage, if you want to be updated, take a look at our contacts.
